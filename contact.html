Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get my car serviced?

It is generally recommended to get your car serviced every 6,000 to 8,000 miles or every 6 months, whichever comes first.

What should I do if my check engine light comes on?

If your check engine light comes on, it's important to have your vehicle inspected by a qualified mechanic to diagnose the issue and avoid potential damage.

How long does it take to replace a car battery?

The time it takes to replace a car battery can vary depending on the make and model of your vehicle, but it typically takes around 30 minutes to an hour.

What are some common signs that my brakes need to be replaced?

Common signs that your brakes may need to be replaced include squeaking or grinding noises, reduced braking performance, and a vibrating brake pedal.
